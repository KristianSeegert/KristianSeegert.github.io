About

I am a postdoctoral researcher in photonics at the Technical University of Denmark. My research focuses on semiconductor photonic devices, nonlinear optical dynamics, nanophotonics, and non-Hermitian photonics. I combine analytical theory, numerical modelling, electromagnetic simulations, device design, and experimental characterization to understand and engineer active nanophotonic systems.

Research Interests

  • Semiconductor lasers
  • Nanophotonics
  • Non-Hermitian photonics
  • Laser dynamics
  • Coupled optical resonators
  • Integrated photonics
  • Nonlinear optics
