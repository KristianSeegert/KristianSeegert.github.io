## About

Welcome to my website.

I am a postdoctoral researcher in photonics at the Technical University of Denmark. My research combines analytical theory, numerical modelling, electromagnetic simulations, device design, and experimental characterization to understand and engineer active nanophotonic systems.

My current research focuses on semiconductor photonic devices, nonlinear optical dynamics, nanophotonics, and non-Hermitian photonics, with particular interests in nanostructured lasers, coupled resonators, and novel laser dynamics.
